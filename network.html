PNRR Projects

The Digital Philology Hub is a pilot that links the needs and requirements of two PNRR projects.

H2IOSC

Humanities and cultural Heritage Italian Open Science Cloud

In the PNRR H2IOSC project, under Working Package 7, entitled 'Community pilots: cross-domain services and innovative environments', the creation of the 'Digital Philology Hub' pilot is planned. Researchers and technologists from the Italian node of the European research infrastructure DARIAH are involved on the design and development of the platform.

CHANGES

Cultural Heritage Active Innovation for Nex-gen Sustainable Society Extended Partnership

The PNRR foundation CHANGES, within the Spoke 3, named 'Digital library, archives and philology' plans the creation of a series of digital tools that support the various phases of philological work. Among the universities and research institutions of the CHANGES foundation, the Scuola Normale Superiore and the OVI Institute of the CNR are particularly involved in the activities necessary for the realisation of the Digital Philology Hub.

Research groups

The research groups involved in the two PNRR projects collaborate in order to achieve the objectives of their pilot project and, consequently, of the Digital Philology Hub. These groups belong to the Italian node of the European Research Infrastructure DARIAH, together with PhD students and researchers from the Scuola Normale Superiore.

DARIAH-IT, CNR OVI

Requirements, design, development

The DARIAH-IT team from the Opera del Vocabolario Italiano - CNR Institute, composed of researchers and technologists, involved in Working Package 7 of the PNRR H2IOSC project.
