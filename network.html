Projects

The Digital Philology Hub is a pilot that links the needs and requirements of two PNRR projects.

  • H2IOSC

    In the PNRR H2IOSC project, under Working Package 7, entitled 'Community pilots: cross-domain services and innovative environments', the creation of the 'Digital Philology Hub' pilot is planned. Researchers and technologists from the Italian node of the European research infrastructure DARIAH are involved in the design and development of the platform.

  • CHANGES

    The PNRR foundation CHANGES, within the Spoke 3, named 'Digital library, archives and philology' plans the creation of a series of digital tools that support the various phases of philological work. Among the universities and research institutions of the CHANGES foundation, the Scuola Normale Superiore and the OVI Institute of the CNR are particularly involved in the activities necessary for the realisation of the Digital Philology Hub.

Groups involved

Working on the collection of requirements and the development of the platform are two working groups:

  • DARIAH-IT
    Web Development

    The team of researchers and technologists of the Italian node of the European research infrastructure DARIAH, involved in the PNRR H2IOSC project.

  • Scuola Normale Superiore
    Philological and linguistic aspects

    The group of researchers and PhD students from Scuola Normale Superiore, involved in the PNRR project CHANGES and the PhD FROID.

Digital Philology Hub

The Digital Philology Hub is a pilot of the PNRR projects CHANGES and H2IOSC that will provide an innovative, interdisciplinary digital environment to support data-driven research in the field of medieval and early modern manuscripts and textual culture studies.
