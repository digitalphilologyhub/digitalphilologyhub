Overview

The project is connected to the PNRR projects PE CHANGES (Cultural Heritage Active Innovation for Next-Gen Sustainable Society), in particular to the WP3 "Creating a digital philology environment and digital libraries of authorised texts" of which the Scuola Normale Superiore is leader with the co-ordination of Professor Lino Leonardi, and PNRR IR H2IOSC (Humanities and cultural Heritage Italian Open Science Cloud), in particular to the WP7 "Community pilots: cross-domain services and innovative environments", coordinated by OVI researcher Emiliano Degl'Innocenti (scientific head of the Italian node of DARIAH).

PhD Program "Filologia Romanza e Italiana Digitale"

The Digital Philology HUB is a pilot of the PNRR projects CHANGES and H2IOSC that will provide an innovative, interdisciplinary digital environment to support data-driven research in the field of medieval and early modern manuscripts and textual culture studies. Two main groups are involved in the development: the group of researchers from the Italian node of the European Research Infrastructure DARIAH and the group of researchers and PhD students from the Scuola Normale Superiore.

  • Innovative cross-domain environment

    Will be developed within the H2IOSC WP7 'Community pilots: innovative cross-domain services and environments'

  • Digital library, archives and philology

    One of the objectives of Spoke 3 'Digital library, archives and philology' of CHANGES.

  • Tools for philological research

    The design of this platform for philological research and the establishment of the PhD course FROID at the Scuola Normale Superiore are two first examples of the fruitful synergy between the PNRR IR H2IOSC and PE CHANGES.

References and preliminary remarks

The pilot starts from the guidelines offered by pubblications and taking into account the reflections arising from various meetings:

  • Lino Leonardi, Filologia digitale del Medioevo italiano, Italianistica digitale «Griseldaonline», 20, 2 (2021), pp. 77-89.
  • Lino Leonardi and Emiliano Degl'Innocenti, Filologia digitale e infrastrutture di ricerca, nel PNRR e oltre (Siena, XII Annual AIUCD Conference La memoria digitale, 5-7 June 2023)
  • The workshop "Filologia digitale e testi italiani antichi - Verso un sistema integrato di ricerca" (Pisa, Scuola Normale Superiore, 27-28 June 2022)
  • XVI International Seminar on Romance Philology Filologia digitale e Medioevo romanzo. Costs, benefits, perspectives (Siena, University for Foreigners, 23-24 October 2023).
