Involved parties

  • The project is connected to the PNRR projects H2IOSC (Humanities and cultural Heritage Italian Open Science Cloud) and CHANGES (Cultural Heritage Active Innovation for Next-Gen Sustainable Society).
  • H2IOSC is involved in the project with WP7 "Community pilots: cross-domain services and innovative environments", coordinated by OVI researcher Emiliano Degl'Innocenti (scientific head of the Italian node of DARIAH).
  • CHANGES is involved in the project with WP3 "Creating a digital philology environment and digital libraries of authorised texts" of which the Scuola Normale Superiore is leader with the co-ordination of Professor Lino Leonardi.
  • Two main groups are involved in the development: the group of researchers from the Italian node of the European Research Infrastructure DARIAH and the group of researchers and PhD students from the Scuola Normale Superiore.
  • The design of this platform for philological research and the establishment of the PhD course FROID at the Scuola Normale Superiore are two first examples of the fruitful synergy between H2IOSC and CHANGES.

References and preliminary remarks

The pilot starts from the guidelines offered by pubblications and taking into account the reflections arising from various meetings:

  • Lino Leonardi, Filologia digitale del Medioevo italiano, Italianistica digitale «Griseldaonline», 20, 2 (2021), pp. 77-89.
  • Lino Leonardi and Emiliano Degl'Innocenti, Filologia digitale e infrastrutture di ricerca, nel PNRR e oltre (Siena, XII Annual AIUCD Conference La memoria digitale, 5-7 June 2023)
  • The workshop "Filologia digitale e testi italiani antichi - Verso un sistema integrato di ricerca" (Pisa, Scuola Normale Superiore, 27-28 June 2022)
  • XVI International Seminar on Romance Philology Filologia digitale e Medioevo romanzo. Costs, benefits, perspectives (Siena, University for Foreigners, 23-24 October 2023).
